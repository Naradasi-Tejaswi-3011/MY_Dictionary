Turbo

Meaning:

Turbo refers to a turbine-driven device that increases engine efficiency and power by forcing extra air into the combustion chamber.

Usage:

  • The sports car's engine is equipped with a turbo.
  • Adding a turbo boosts acceleration.
  • He bought a turbo bike last week.
